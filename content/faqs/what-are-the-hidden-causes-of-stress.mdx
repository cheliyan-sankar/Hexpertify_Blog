question: What are the hidden causes of stress?
answer: >-
  Stress is not always caused by obvious pressures. Sometimes it develops from
  everyday patterns such as constantly staying busy, avoiding difficult
  conversations, taking on too much responsibility, neglecting personal needs,
  or continuing routines that no longer feel meaningful. These patterns can
  become so familiar that their impact often goes unnoticed.
