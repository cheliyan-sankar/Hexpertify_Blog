question: Why am I always stressed even when life seems fine?
answer: >-
  Stress is not always caused by obvious problems or major life events.
  Sometimes it is the result of habits, expectations, responsibilities, and
  situations that have become so familiar that they go unnoticed. Continually
  carrying more than you can sustain, avoiding important conversations,
  neglecting your own needs, or pursuing goals that no longer feel meaningful
  can all contribute to stress, even when life appears fine on the surface.
