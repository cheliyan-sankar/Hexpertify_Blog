question: Why do I struggle to say no?
answer: >-
  People often struggle to say no because they feel responsible for others, want
  to avoid disappointing people, or have become accustomed to being the reliable
  one. Over time, automatically saying yes can become a habit, making it
  difficult to recognize personal limits and creating additional emotional
  strain.
