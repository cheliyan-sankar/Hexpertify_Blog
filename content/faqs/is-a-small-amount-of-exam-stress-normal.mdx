question: Is a small amount of exam stress normal?
answer: >-
  Yes, a small amount of exam stress is normal and can even be helpful. Mild
  stress can increase alertness and motivation, helping students stay focused on
  their studies. However, when stress becomes excessive and begins to affect
  sleep, concentration, or emotional balance, it stops being helpful and starts
  interfering with learning and performance.
