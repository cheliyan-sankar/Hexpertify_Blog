question: Why do I feel emotionally drained all the time?
answer: >-
  Feeling emotionally drained is not always the result of a single stressful
  event. It can develop gradually through ongoing responsibilities, unresolved
  concerns, constant busyness, and the habit of prioritizing everyone else's
  needs before your own. Over time, carrying more than you can sustainably
  manage can leave you feeling depleted.
