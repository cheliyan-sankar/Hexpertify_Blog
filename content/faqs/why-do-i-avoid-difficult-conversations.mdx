question: Why do I avoid difficult conversations?
answer: >-
  Many people avoid difficult conversations because conflict feels
  uncomfortable. In the short term, staying silent may seem easier. However,
  unspoken concerns, unmet needs, and unresolved frustrations rarely disappear.
  The emotional effort required to continually avoid these conversations can
  become a source of ongoing stress.
