question: Why do students procrastinate during exams?
answer: >-
  Students often procrastinate because the brain tries to avoid difficult or
  stressful tasks. Instead of studying challenging topics, they may engage in
  easier activities such as organizing notes, cleaning their desk, or using
  their phone. These actions create a temporary sense of productivity or reward,
  making it harder to return to studying.
