question: How can students create a healthier study routine during exams?
answer: >-
  A healthier study routine involves breaking large tasks into smaller and
  manageable goals. Instead of focusing on completing many chapters at once,
  students can study in short time blocks or micro-goals such as studying for 40
  minutes at a time. Combining focused study sessions with short breaks, proper
  sleep, and balanced daily habits helps maintain both productivity and mental
  well-being.
