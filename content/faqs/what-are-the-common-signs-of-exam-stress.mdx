question: What are the common signs of exam stress?
answer: >-
  Common signs of exam stress usually appear gradually through everyday
  behaviours. Students may experience excessive phone usage, procrastination,
  difficulty starting tasks, headaches, poor sleep, brain fog, and constant
  tiredness. Stress can also show through physical symptoms such as stomach
  distress or fatigue.
