question: How can parents support students during exam time?
answer: >-
  Parents can support students by creating a calm and supportive environment
  during exam periods. Instead of focusing only on marks or preparation, asking
  questions such as “How are you feeling today?” helps students feel understood.
  Emotional validation and encouragement are more helpful than constant
  reminders about performance. A peaceful home environment allows students to
  stay focused and confident.
