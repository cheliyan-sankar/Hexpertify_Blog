question: Why does rest make me feel guilty?
answer: >-
  For some people, busyness becomes so familiar that productivity begins to feel
  linked to self-worth. When this happens, moments of rest may feel
  uncomfortable or undeserved. Over time, the habit of constantly staying busy
  can make recovery feel unnatural, even when it is necessary.
