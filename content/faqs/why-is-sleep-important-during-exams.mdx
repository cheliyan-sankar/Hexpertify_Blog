question: Why is sleep important during exams?
answer: >-
  Sleep plays an essential role in memory consolidation, which is the process by
  which the brain converts newly learned information into long-term memory. When
  students reduce sleep or stay awake all night to study, the brain cannot
  properly store information. As a result, concentration, focus, and performance
  decrease. Well-rested studying is therefore more effective than studying
  longer hours without sleep.
